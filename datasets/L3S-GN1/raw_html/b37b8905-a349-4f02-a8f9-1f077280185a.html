Associated Press - December 29, 2007 4:44 AM ET

BOSTON (AP) - The city of Boston is gearing up for its 32nd annual First Night celebration.

The New Year's Eve party takes place in locations across the city beginning early in the afternoon Monday and culminating with a fireworks show over Boston Harbor.

Mayor Tom Menino says First Night celebrates the city's culture and traditions.

1 of the highlights will include elaborate ice sculptures set up in places like Boston Common, Copley Square and the New England Aquarium.

Performances will takes place at over 40 outdoor and indoor venues.

The event first began in 1976 by artists who were looking for an alternative to traditional New Year's Eve revelry.

It was has since been copied by other cities around the country.
