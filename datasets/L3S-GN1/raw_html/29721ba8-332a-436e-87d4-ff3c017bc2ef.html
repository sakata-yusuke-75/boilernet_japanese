Gang member taunts police in video

   

Miami, Florida -  A reputed gang member is now in jail in Miami for showing off his guns and taunting police on an Internet video.

?King Bird Road? Rudy Villanueva and another alleged gang member were arrested today on federal weapons charges. Investigators say they were incensed when they saw the video and heard the threats.

Villanueva and Anthony Logan are seen in the video brandishing an AK-47 assault rifle, a shotgun and various handguns. The two are heard making threats against the Miami-Dade gang unit.

Villanueva has a long criminal history and three prior felony convictions. He's the alleged leader of the Bird Road Boys and Logan is a purported member of the gang in an area southwest of Miami.

Both suspects made brief initial appearances today in federal court to have lawyers appointed for them and set future court dates. Prosecutors are asking that both men be held without bail until trial.
